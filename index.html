Our Mission

Our Church’s Mission is to help and care for one another as we strive to be an example of Christ’s love to all. (Read John 13:34-35)

Our Church Covenant

Having been led, as we believe, by the Spirit of God to receive Jesus, The Christ, as our Savior and Lord, we do now solemnly and joyfully covenant with each other, to walk together with Him in Christian love, We do therefore pray… That we might watch over one another with Christian love, remembering each other in prayer, and aiding each other in sickness and distress. That we will regularly assemble for the public worship of God, and the observance of the ordinances of the Church. That we will maintain daily family and personal devotions in our home, provide for the religious training of our children and those under our care for the service of Christ and His people. That we will seek Divine aid to enable us to affirm love, justice and the unity of all people and to work as faithful disciples of The Christ. That we cheerfully contribute of our property according as God has prospered us for the maintenance of a faithful and evangelical ministry among us, for the relief of the distressed and for spreading The Gospel to all nations. And that we will strive to live to the glory of God who has given us life, and who has called us to serve Him and our sisters and brothers in the name of His Son.
